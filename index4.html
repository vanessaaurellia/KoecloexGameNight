The Portkey has transported you and your friends.

You are all in a dim room with a small cot. It feels cold and damp. You hear the lapping of water and there are bars on one wall. Are you in Azkaban?

You and your friends slide open the barred door and begin walking around the building. There are rows and levels of cells for prisoners but you don't see any Dementors.

As you make your way outside, you find a sign that tells where you are - Alcatraz? Your roommate tells you that it is a famous muggle prison in the United States that was supposed to be impossible to escape from, but now it is a park that people visit and tour during the day.

The sun is setting

And you can see a boat leaving the small island. It must have been the last tour group for the day. You and your roommates head back inside to explore and and see what else you can find.

You find a large room and a sign tells you that this was the prison library when it held inmates long ago. In it, you find empty bookshelves, a map, and papers with weather information on them. The weather papers list the highest and lowest recorded temperatures for each state in the USA.

More locks

You continue to explore and only come across one locked area. Another cell area with combination locks.

As it grows darker you notice a blue glow coming from outside. You all decide to go check it out.

You follow the light to a building.

But the building is barely a building at all.
The walls are mostly crumbled.
The roof is gone.
